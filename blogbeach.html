    Pondicherry – The French Part of India

    Having a strong French influence, Pondicherry is a destination for lovers. The town is filled with ancient architectures and beautiful beaches. Often referred to as the French Riviera, it is one of the seven Union Territories of India. Pondicherry gives the French feel even though it is far away from France and so does its beaches. Blessed with numerous pristine beaches, Pondicherry offers some amazing romantic evenings.
